Pasta Faggioli


Pasta e fagioli. Source: www.tavolartegusto.it

This pasta fagioli will blow your mind and your colon! Literally!
Let's see how it's prepared

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Fagioli
  • Tomato sauce
  • Amore!

Preparation

  1. Put pasta to boil
  2. Put fagioli to boil
  3. Add all in a pan with some oil and garlic and tomato sauce
  4. Put your Amore into it
  5. Pronto!
